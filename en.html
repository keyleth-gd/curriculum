# Projects

# Stateless

A competitive analogic game inspired on mercenaries, where each player controls a group of mercenaries that need to fulfill contracts to become the most famous group. The game is based on mechanics like Deck Building, Missions, and Auction. The project was developed in a group at Faculdade Mèliés, and my primary role was Game Design.

# Conectando a Hora do Break

A game where an intern takes the mission of improving his work environment by helping other employees to handle their problems. He manages to do it through "combats" that are turn-based conversations where the character can use arguments to modify the employees' emotions. This project was developed in a group at Faculdade Mèliés, where I worked as Game Designer.

# Does it feel like home?

A game that follows Isaac through the process of turning his new house into a home. The game is a point-and-click where the play must find items that will activate Isaac's memories. This project was developed during a Game Jam promoted by Professor Lia in the Software Engineer class at Faculdade Méliès. In this project, I worked with Game Design and Programming.

# I Miss You

I Miss You is a visual novel developed for the Women Game Jam 2020 that tells the history of Sissi and her life away from her friends. The project was made with three more people, where I was responsible for Programming and Game Design.

# Alquixinim

Alquixinim is a 2D action game where two players compete for the philosopher's stone. They must traverse a labyrinth filled with traps having at their disposal abilities like dash, energy blasts, and some power-ups that can be found throughout the map.

# Synapses

Synapses is a storytelling puzzle game where a girl wakes up in a hospital room with a doctor and a police officer. However, she lost her memories. Now the player must create synapses to recover these memories and answer the questions being made by the officer. This game was developed for Global Game Jam 2021, where I acted as Game Designer.

# Oasis

Oasis is a 3D adventure game where the player commands Lina, a desert survivor who is trapped in a hostile environment and needs to find her people's heritage and a way to continue her journey.
